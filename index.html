Fat-Loss + Muscle-Retention Diet Plan

Goal: Lose 3.5–4 kg in 30 days while retaining & building muscle.
Workout Time: 5:30 PM – 7:30 PM
Total Calories: ~1550–1650 kcal | Protein: ~135g | Carbs: ~120g | Fats: ~45g

8:00 AM – Meal 1: Breakfast

  • Rolled oats – 30g
  • Low-fat milk – 120ml
  • Banana – 100g
  • Mixed seeds (chia, flax, pumpkin, sunflower) – 20g total
  • Date – 1 (8g)
  • Whey protein – 30g
~400 kcal | 30g protein | 45g carbs | 12g fat

11:00 AM – Meal 2: Snack

  • Boiled egg – 1
  • Egg white – 1
  • Black coffee or green tea
~100 kcal | 9g protein | 1g carbs | 6g fat

2:00 PM – Meal 3: Lunch

  • Chicken breast – 120g
  • Cooked quinoa – 75g
  • Vegetable sabzi – 100g
  • Salad – 50g
~400 kcal | 35g protein | 30g carbs | 12g fat

4:30 PM – Meal 4: Pre-Workout Snack

  • Banana or Apple – 100g
  • Almonds – 10g (6 pieces)
  • Green tea or lemon water
~150 kcal | 3g protein | 20g carbs | 6g fat

Immediately Post-Workout

  • Whey protein – 30g in water
~120 kcal | 24g protein | 2g carbs | 1g fat

8:00 PM – Meal 5: Dinner

  • Paneer (low-fat) or tofu – 100g
  • Stir-fried veggies – 100–150g
  • Optional: Multigrain roti – 30g dough
~350–400 kcal | 25–30g protein | 20g carbs | 15g fat

9:30 PM – Optional Night Add-On

  • ½ scoop whey OR warm turmeric milk – 100ml
~80–100 kcal | 12g protein | 2g carbs | 1g fat
